This is my digital garden.

A digital garden is a collection of evolving ideas that aren't strictly organised by their publication date.

The garden is composed of plants. Each plant represents a single idea. Navigate to other plants to explore the garden. The overview lists all plants.

My digital garden is built using HTML and CSS. Intentionally without the use of JavaScript.

The garden can be viewed in a light and dark color scheme (I highly recommend the light color scheme). The color scheme automatically adjusts to the browser's color scheme preference.
